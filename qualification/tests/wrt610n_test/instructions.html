WRT610n Test

When prompted to hard-reset the WRT610n:

  1. Unplug the power
  2. Push the reset button in with the pin
  3. Plug in the power while holding in the reset button
  4. When the light on the front turns purple, release the reset button.

Test Setup

Plug the WRT610n into one of the 4 ethernet jacks. NOT the jack labeled internet and then plug in the power.

Press 'Continue' to proceed.
